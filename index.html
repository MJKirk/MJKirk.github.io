Intro

I am currently a postdoc at the Institute of Cosmos Sciences of the University of Barcelona (ICCUB). See my bio for more history.

My research in focused on particle physics, studying the Standard Model and beyond!

A list of my publications can be found in the INSPIRE database here. You can also check my INSPIRE profile or my ORCID profile.

Research Interests

I am interested in investigating anomalies in the flavour sector, and how they might be connected to new physics. I am also interested in improving the precision of the theory predictions in flavour physics, in order to better distinguish between the Standard Model and New Physics. Some of my current projects include looking at naturalness of new physics models for flavour anomalies, and working to improve less well known theory parameters for Bs mixing, while I have previously worked on the overlap of dark matter models and flavour and model independent BSM effects in quark flavour physics.
